THE SECOND CHANCE STANDARD™

A documented path from access to delivery.

Secure & Assess

Confirm authorized access and safety first, then document the actual condition and scope.

PHASES 3–4

PHASES 5–6

Report out-of-scope repair findings and complete the detailed cleaning work.

PHASES 7–8

Verify & Deliver

Complete quality control, final photos, management approval, and client-ready closeout.
